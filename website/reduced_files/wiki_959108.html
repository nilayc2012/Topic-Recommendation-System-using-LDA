Boy seaman

Wikipedia Link

A boy seaman (plural boy seamen) is a boy (male minor) who serves as seaman and/or is trained for such service. Royal Navy. In the British naval forces, where there was a need to recruit enough hands to man the vast fleet of the British Empire, extensive regulations existed concerning the selection and status of boys enlisted to keep filling the ranks.
